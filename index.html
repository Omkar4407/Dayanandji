About Us

NEWays Bharat Federation is a national initiative focused on uplifting communities through grassroots movements in education, science, entrepreneurship, and social reform. Founded with a vision to bridge traditional values and modern innovation, the organization is committed to building a sustainable and empowered India.

Education & Youth Development

We believe education is the foundation for empowerment. Through institutions like Govind Bal Mandir and active learning projects, we've mentored youth across India, organized field trips, trained teachers, and promoted curiosity-led innovation among school children.

Science, Innovation & Rural Tech

Partnering with Vigyan Bharati and other networks, we've hosted lectures on ancient sciences, organized innovation exhibitions, and empowered entrepreneurs in villages to use science for scalable impact.
